About Us

Warm, stylish, and made for a great London pub night.

Ox & Gate brings together the classic pub atmosphere with a more polished, modern feel. Whether you are dropping in for drinks, dinner, a casual catch-up, or a private gathering, it is designed to feel welcoming and memorable.

Located in Cricklewood, Ox & Gate offers a great place to unwind, meet friends, and enjoy a proper local pub setting.
